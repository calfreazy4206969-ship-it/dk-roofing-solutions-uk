Guttering in Maypole

The 1980s–90s uPVC guttering on Maypole's post-war properties is now reaching the end of its life — joints brittle, brackets failing, fascia boards rotting behind. Full guttering and fascia replacement is usually a half-day job on a typical Maypole semi and makes a significant difference to the roofline's weather resistance.

Left too long, failing guttering causes damp patches on external walls and, eventually, water ingress into the structure. Being based in Kings Heath — about 2 miles from Maypole — means we can survey guttering jobs quickly and give you a fixed price without delay.

Signs you need new guttering

  • Damp patches on the wall below the gutterline
  • Guttering overflowing during heavy rain
  • Visible cracks in uPVC sections
  • Sagging gutter sections
  • Rotten or broken fascia board behind the gutter
  • Gutters pulling away from the wall
