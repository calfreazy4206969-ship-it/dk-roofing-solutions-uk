Local Knowledge

Guttering in Moseley B13

Cast iron guttering is common on Moseley's Victorian and Edwardian properties — and while it can outlast uPVC when properly maintained, it corrodes at the joints and at wall fixings over time. We repair cast iron where possible, and replace section-by-section where it's past saving.

Moseley's large trees are another factor — leaf debris builds up in gutters quickly and blocked gutters are a frequent cause of damp in the terraced properties along the main roads. Regular clearing prevents water from backing up and overflowing behind the fascia.

Signs you need guttering work

  • Damp on external wall below gutterline
  • Overflowing in heavy rain
  • Rust or cracks in cast iron
  • Sagging or loose sections
  • Rotten fascia behind gutter
  • Gutters pulling away from the wall
