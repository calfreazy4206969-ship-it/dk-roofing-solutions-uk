Guttering & Fascia Services in Yardley Wood B14

Most of Yardley Wood's 1930s–50s properties have uPVC guttering fitted from the 1980s onwards. That uPVC is now 30–40 years old — joints brittle, brackets failing, fascia boards behind it starting to rot. Full gutter and fascia replacement is usually a half-day job on a typical Yardley Wood semi, and makes a real difference to the long-term weather resistance of the roofline.

uPVC Gutter Replacement

Full replacement of failed uPVC guttering runs — including joints, brackets and corners. We match profiles to existing where possible, or upgrade to a wider system for improved flow.

Fascia & Soffit Boards

Rotten timber fascia boards are common on Yardley Wood's older properties. We replace with low-maintenance uPVC fascias and soffits — factory finished, never needs painting.

Gutter Clearing

Moss debris from sloping roofs, leaves and silt block uPVC gutters. We clear, flush and check fall angles — a fast job that prevents damp problems below.

Downpipe Repair

Cracked or leaking downpipes, blocked offsets and failed joints at the shoe — repaired or replaced as needed. We check the connection to the underground drainage run.

Seamless Aluminium

For Yardley Wood's larger detached properties, seamless aluminium guttering is a premium option — extruded on-site to the exact run length, no joints, no leaks.

Roof Edge Detailing

Correct drip edge and lead flashing at the eaves keeps water off the fascia and into the gutter. We check and correct the roof edge detail as part of any full gutter replacement.
