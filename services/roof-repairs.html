Don't Ignore These

Signs You Need a Roof Repair

Caught early, most repairs are quick and affordable. Left too long, small problems become expensive ones.

💧

Water stains on ceilings

Brown or damp patches on ceilings are almost always a sign of a roof leak above.

🪨

Missing or broken tiles

A single displaced tile exposes the felt beneath and lets water in within weeks.

🌿

Moss & algae growth

Heavy moss holds moisture against tiles and breaks down the surface over time.

🔩

Cracked or loose flashings

Flashings around chimneys, skylights and valleys are a common entry point for leaks.

🧱

Crumbling mortar on ridge

Ridge tiles bedded in old mortar loosen over time — they can fall off in high winds.

🌫️

Damp or musty loft

Persistent damp smell in your loft often means water is getting in at a small point above.
