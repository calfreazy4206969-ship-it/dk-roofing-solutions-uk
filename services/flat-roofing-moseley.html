Local Knowledge

Flat Roofing in Moseley B13

Moseley's Victorian terraces have a high density of bay windows — and bay window flat roofs need periodic maintenance. Many were originally lead-detailed, with later felt replacements that are now ageing. We install GRP or EPDM on bay window roofs with correct lead edge detailing to maintain the look of the property.

Rear kitchen extensions are the other flat roofing hotspot in Moseley. Many of the extensions built in the 70s and 80s used traditional felt that is now well beyond its lifespan. We replace these with EPDM rubber — seamless, UV-resistant and suitable for low-pitch applications where felt struggles.

Signs your flat roof needs replacing

  • Bubbling or blistering felt
  • Ponding water 48+ hours
  • Splits or cracks in the covering
  • Damp patches below
  • Felt pulling from upstands
  • 15+ years old
