Don't Ignore These

Signs Your Flat Roof Needs Attention

Flat roof problems rarely fix themselves. Catching them early saves you significantly on repair costs.

💧

Pooling water

Standing water after rain means drainage is failing or the surface has dipped.

🫧

Bubbling or blistering

Bubbles in the membrane mean trapped moisture — the surface is failing from below.

🔍

Cracks or splits

Visible splits in felt or membrane allow water straight through to the structure.

🌿

Moss & algae

Growth holds moisture on the surface and accelerates deterioration of the felt.

🏠

Cold rooms below

A failing flat roof loses insulation value — rooms beneath become noticeably colder.

🌊

Damp stains inside

Ceiling stains directly below a flat roof almost always point to a membrane failure.

What We Do

Flat Roofing Services We Cover

EPDM Rubber Flat Roofing

We install fully bonded EPDM rubber systems — a single seamless membrane that handles UV, temperature change and movement far better than felt. Lifespan of 25–50 years with minimal maintenance.

⚠️ If old felt is left

Traditional felt typically lasts 10–15 years. Once it starts to crack and blister, water gets under it fast. Replacing it before it fully fails saves the deck boards and insulation below — replacing those adds significantly to the job cost.

From £1,200

GRP Fibreglass Flat Roofing

GRP (glass reinforced plastic) creates a rigid, seamless waterproof shell — ideal for roofs with complex shapes, upstands or penetrations. Extremely durable with a 25+ year lifespan and a clean, finished look.

⚠️ If ignored

GRP failures typically start at junctions and upstands. Water enters along the edges before spreading beneath the deck. By the time a damp patch appears inside, the roof deck and joists may already be damaged — turning a surface repair into a structural job.

From £1,400

Flat Roof Repairs

Cracks, splits, lifted edges and failed flashings repaired properly — not just taped over. We diagnose where water is entering and fix the actual cause, not just the visible symptom.

⚠️ If left unrepaired

A small split in a flat roof membrane can allow litres of water into the structure every time it rains. Flat roofs have nowhere for that water to go — it saturates insulation, rots deck boards and eventually soaks through to ceilings. Repair costs double or triple the longer it is left.

From £150

Felt Flat Roof Replacement

When felt has reached the end of its life, we strip it back completely and replace it — either with new high-performance felt or upgrade to EPDM or GRP for a far longer-lasting result.

⚠️ If patches are used instead

Patching aged felt is a short-term fix. The underlying material continues to degrade and new failures appear nearby. Multiple patch repairs over a few years typically cost more than a single full replacement — and the roof still ends up needing replacing.

From £800

Garage Roof Replacement

Garage flat roofs are among the most common flat roof jobs in Birmingham. We replace the full roof — stripping back to bare timber, replacing any rotten deck boards, and laying a new long-life membrane.

⚠️ If left too long

A leaking garage roof will rot the roof joists over time. Once the structural timbers are affected, the job price increases substantially. Replacing the membrane alone when the deck is still sound costs half what it does once the structure needs work too.

From £1,200

Extension & Outbuilding Roofs

Single-storey extension flat roofs, kitchen roofs, utility room roofs — all installed or replaced. We work around existing structures and match the surrounding roofline neatly.

⚠️ If delayed

Extension roofs often sit above living spaces — kitchens, living rooms, bedrooms. A failing membrane here leads directly to ceiling damage and mould in a main room. Interior damage from a flat roof leak typically costs more to fix than the roof itself.

From £2,400

Bay Window Flat Roofs

Bay window flat roofs are small but awkward — lots of angles, abutments and lead flashings. We replace the covering and re-lead all junctions properly to stop the most common source of bay window damp.

⚠️ If ignored

Bay window leaks travel sideways inside the wall structure before they appear as damp inside. By the time a stain shows on a bay window ceiling or wall, water may have been tracking through the frame for months, causing hidden rot and mould behind plasterwork.

From £400

Drainage & Falls Correction

Pooling water on a flat roof is a drainage problem. We re-grade the falls using tapered insulation or deck build-up, add or reposition outlets, and ensure water leaves the roof completely after every rainfall.

⚠️ If standing water is ignored

Flat roofs are designed to drain, not hold water. Persistent ponding adds structural load, accelerates membrane breakdown and keeps the roof saturated. Even a waterproof membrane degrades faster under permanent standing water — and if the membrane ever fails, the volume of water already sitting on the roof pours in at once.
