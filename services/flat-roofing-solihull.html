Flat Roofing in Solihull B91 / B92

Solihull's larger detached properties — particularly in Olton, Shirley and the Knowle area — commonly have double or triple garages with flat roofs. These are larger jobs than a typical Birmingham terrace extension, and they require care to get the drainage and detailing right on a bigger roof area.

We use EPDM rubber for larger flat roofs — a seamless, UV-stable membrane that handles the greater surface areas and pooling risks associated with bigger flat roofs. GRP is available where the customer prefers a harder surface. We provide itemised written quotes breaking down material and labour costs separately.

Signs your flat roof needs replacing

  • Bubbling or blistering on the felt surface
  • Ponding water 48+ hours after rain
  • Splits or cracks in the covering
  • Damp ceiling below the roof
  • Felt pulling away from upstands
  • 15+ years old — lifespan exceeded
