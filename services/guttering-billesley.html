Local Knowledge

Guttering in Billesley B13

Billesley's 1930s semis often retain original cast iron guttering — or had it replaced with early uPVC in the 1980s. Both are now ageing. Cast iron fails at the joints and at wall brackets; the early uPVC has become brittle. Full gutter replacement is the right solution where the run is failing in multiple places — and we can replace fascia boards at the same time where they've rotted behind the gutter.

When we replace gutters, we always check the roof edge at the same time. Billesley's older properties sometimes have failing mortar at the verge, or felt that has lifted at the eaves — and it's far cheaper to address these while the scaffolding or ladders are already in position.

Signs your guttering needs attention

  • Damp on wall below gutter
  • Overflow in heavy rain
  • Cast iron rust streaks
  • Cracked or split uPVC
  • Sagging gutter sections
  • Rotten fascia board
