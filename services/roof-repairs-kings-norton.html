Roof Repairs in B30 & B38

Kings Norton has an unusual housing mix even by Birmingham standards. The village centre area around the Green has genuinely old properties — cottages and terraces from the 18th and 19th centuries — alongside Victorian semis and terraces. Further out, the post-war estates of the 1950s–70s dominate, with the standard concrete interlocking tile roofs of that era.

This means we work on a wider range of roofing problems in Kings Norton than in most areas — from natural slate repairs and chimney repointing on the village properties to straightforward concrete tile replacement on post-war semis.
