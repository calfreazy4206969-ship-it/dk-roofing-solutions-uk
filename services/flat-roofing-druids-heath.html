Flat Roofing in Druids Heath

Flat-roofed garages are a defining feature of Druids Heath's estate layout — both private single garages and communal garage blocks. The original felt on these roofs has long since exceeded its lifespan, and many are now actively leaking or close to it. EPDM rubber is the ideal replacement — a seamless membrane that handles the larger flat areas common in estate garage layouts, with a 25–50 year lifespan that far exceeds traditional felt. Most single garage roof replacements can be completed in a day.

Signs your flat roof needs attention

  • Felt bubbling or blistering
  • Ponding water after rain
  • Cracks in the covering
  • Damp ceiling inside garage or room below
  • Felt pulling away from edges
  • 15+ years old (well past lifespan)
