Signs your flat roof needs replacing

  • Bubbling or blistering felt
  • Ponding water 48+ hours after rain
  • Visible cracks or splits in the surface
  • Damp ceiling or staining below
  • Felt pulling away from upstands
  • Roof is 15+ years old
