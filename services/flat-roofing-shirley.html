EPDM Rubber Roofs

Single-ply rubber membrane with a 25–50 year lifespan. Seamless, UV-stable and ideal for larger flat roof areas where pooling is a risk.

GRP Fibreglass

Glass-reinforced plastic roofing — rigid, seamless and extremely durable. 25+ year lifespan, available where a harder surface is preferred.

Garage Roofs

Garage flat roof replacements are one of the most common jobs on Shirley properties. We replace felt systems in EPDM or GRP — properly drained and detailed.

Extension Flat Roofs

Rear kitchen and utility extensions — proper falls, upstand detailing and long-life membranes. Not a patch-over job.

Porch & Bay Roofs

Porch and bay window flat roofs repaired or replaced. Smaller areas suited to GRP for a clean, rigid finish.

Felt Replacement

Tired of patching old felt? We remove it entirely and install a modern long-life system — EPDM or GRP — that won't need patching again.
