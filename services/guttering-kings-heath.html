Local Knowledge

Guttering in B14

Kings Heath's Victorian terraces have predominantly cast iron guttering — heavy, prone to rust at the joints, and often cracked at the brackets after years of freeze-thaw. Many homeowners replace it with modern uPVC, though we can also repair and repaint cast iron where the customer wants to keep the original look.

The 1930s semis further out on the B14 roads typically have uPVC fitted in the 1980s and 90s which is now brittle and starting to fail at the joints. Full gutter replacement is usually a half-day job on a typical semi.

Being based locally means we can survey guttering jobs quickly and give you a fixed price without making a special trip.

Signs you need new guttering

  • Damp patches on the external wall below the gutterline
  • Guttering overflowing during heavy rain
  • Visible rust or cracks in cast iron gutters
  • Loose or sagging gutter sections
  • Gutters pulling away from the fascia
  • Rotten or broken fascia board behind the gutter
