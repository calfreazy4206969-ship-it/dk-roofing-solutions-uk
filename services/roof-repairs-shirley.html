Cracked Tile Replacement

Cracked, delaminating or broken tiles replaced on a like-for-like basis — concrete or clay, matched to the existing roof as closely as possible.

Lead Valley Repairs

Lead valley failures on Shirley's larger detached properties — repaired or replaced with new code lead or GRP valley liners, properly dressed and weatherproofed.

Hipped Roof Re-bedding

Hip tiles on 1930s–50s semis need periodic re-bedding as the mortar cracks and loosens. We use flexible mortar — no brittle sand/cement that fails in a season.

Ridge Re-bedding

Ridge mortar fails over time on all property types. We strip and re-bed ridge tiles using flexible mortar for a long-lasting repair.

Flashing Repairs

Chimney flashings, abutment details and dormer flashings repaired or replaced — common failure points on Shirley's older and larger properties.

Leak Diagnosis

Not sure where the leak is coming from? We carry out a proper inspection and trace the source — then give you a clear written quote for the repair.
