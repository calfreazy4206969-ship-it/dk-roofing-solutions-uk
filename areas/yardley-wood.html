Roofing in Yardley Wood

Yardley Wood is a well-kept residential area of south Birmingham, bordered by Hall Green to the north and Billesley to the west. The housing is predominantly 1930s–50s semi-detached properties — similar in character to the neighbouring areas — with some post-war bungalows and detached houses on the outer roads.

The common theme across Yardley Wood roofing call-outs is age: the concrete tiles on 1930s properties have often been replaced once already (in the 1960s–70s), and those replacements are now themselves 50–60 years old. The flat felt roofs on rear extensions — added from the 1970s onwards — are another consistent source of work in the area.
