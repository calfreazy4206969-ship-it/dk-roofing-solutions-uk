Roofing in Kings Norton

Kings Norton covers a broad area from the historic village green southward into B38. The housing varies significantly — Victorian terraces and semis near the older parts of the village, 1950s and 60s council-built estates throughout B38, and more modern infill development on former industrial sites.

The older properties typically need ridge tile repointing and slate repairs. The post-war estates, built with flat or low-pitched roofs on rear additions, commonly need flat roof replacements. We carry both pitched and flat roofing materials on the van for most jobs in this area.

Common roof issues in B30 / B38

  • Worn ridge and hip tile mortar
  • Slipped concrete or clay roof tiles
  • Old felt garage and extension flat roofs
  • Lead valley and flashing failures
  • Flat-roofed mid-century homes with pooling water
  • Sagging or cracked plastic guttering
